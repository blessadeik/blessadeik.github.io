What Noise Control Engineers Do

Noise control engineering is a specialized discipline at the intersection of physics, acoustics, mechanical engineering, and public health. These professionals design quieter products, environments, and systems â€” improving life for millions.

Whether working in product design, urban planning, environmental consulting, or research, noise control engineers bring measurable improvements to the soundscapes we all share.
